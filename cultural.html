Cultural Perspective

Apple Vision Pro helps in everyday life by providing convenient access to information, notifications, apps and other features right in front of the user. It can be used for message reading, card viewing, smart home management, video viewing and more without requiring constant use of a smartphone or tablet.

Watching movies

Vision Pro supports HDR and 4K resolution, so can replace the modern TV. At least for single viewing. And it can run both regular and 3D movies. Including for adults.

You can choose an environment that matches the theme of the film. For example, while watching the next series on «Star Wars» (Star Wars), you can be transported to the Tatooine Desert.

Watching sports

The Apple helmet allows you to display windows with additional information about the current competition. In addition to the main screen with the match, race or sparring, you can see the track at a different angle, follow the game that goes parallel, read statistics and so on. It feels like watching sports events to a new level. Among the applications for Vision Pro there are those that focus on fans of a particular sport. An excellent example is the PGA Tour Vision, a highly interactive golf encyclopedia.

Working with Apple apps

Vision Pro supports working with multiple programs simultaneously. And the desktop is the entire apartment - you can place windows with applications anywhere. The helmet remembers where they should be and doesn’t close them until you want it to. The result is a good alternative to several monitors at once. And you can use them even during trips. For this special mode Travel Mode is provided.

Become a Designer Yourself

Use advanced augmented reality (AR) capabilities to seamlessly combine virtual elements with the real world. Visualize your interior design ideas using your eyes and hands. The VisionOS application allows you to change the interior design in real time by decorating and decorating them virtually.

Night vision

With the right settings, Vision Pro allows you to navigate in complete darkness. Of course, we do not have a complete replacement for night vision devices, but the first step in this direction. Recall that the cameras on the phones, too, were primitive at first, but over time, drove from the mass market full-fledged cameras. Today, only professionals buy them.
